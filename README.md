# Space Debris Collection System

## About the project
I came up with an idea of space debris collection system using electromagnetic gripper devices tied by a metallic thread to a medium-size spacecraft that can store a scalable number, estimated to be around 10,000 gripper devicse, based on preliminary mass budget. The medium-size spacecraft can be charged by the space station, or a big solar panel folded by origami method will be unfolded to generate power to the spacecraft which provide power to the gripper devices. The gripper device has magnets to attracts the metal debris in the space. after collecting these debris to at least 1-10 tonnes each time, it is then diverted towards a designated graveyard orbit or asteroid, pending further trajectory analysis (using the method of centrepetal force).

### Spaceship
The spaceship will carry scalable number of tiny-size gripper rocket, launched on a standard rocket to the space. An origami solar panel is folded in the spaceship and will be released in space after it goes into orbit. Solar power will be the main source of energy to keep the maneuverability of the spaceship. Each tiny rocket is tied by a very long cable from the spaceship to make sure sufficient power is supplied to the tiny rocket. An AI will be the master control which monitors the location of all the gripper mechanisms and navigates the orbits of the spaceship.

### Gripper Mechanism
The gripper mechanisms are controlled and navigated by the AI system embedded in a microprocessor (TinyML). During important situations or emergencies, the tiny rocket control will switch to manual mode, where professionals, students or even gamers will handle the situation, such as encountering abnormal shape debris, malfunction of AI, complex orbits, etc. The gripper attracts space debris using electromagnetic force, and can extend from the tiny rocket, tied by a metalltic cable. 

### Communication from the spaceship to the ground station
To solve the serious latency of telecommunication signal transmission from space to the ground station on the earth, my current thought is to place the spaceship is mid- to low orbit, where it transmits signals to mid-to low orbit satellite to minimize the latency to 50 - 240 ms, depending on satellite relay topology.

### Impact to Society
After the whole product chain and technologies are well-developed, it can be an educational kit for university students, graduates, PHDs, and even the public, to experiences the fun of controlling a device in space, watch real-time space view, and collect space debris to contribute to the sustainable development of human society.
